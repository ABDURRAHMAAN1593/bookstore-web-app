HORROR

   THE EXORCIST

~BY WILLIAM PETER BLATTY


The Exorcist is a 1971 horror novel by American
writer William Peter Blatty. The book details the demonic
possession of eleven-year-old Regan MacNeil,
the daughter of a famous actress, and the
two priests who attempt to exorcise the demon.
   THE SHINING

~BY STEPHEN KING


Jack and his family move into an isolated hotel with a
violent past. Living in isolation, Jack begins to lose his
sanity, which affects his family members.
   IT

~BY STEPHEN KING


It is a 1986 horror novel by American author Stephen King.
It was his 22nd book and his 17th novel written
under his own name. The story follows the
experiences of seven children as they are terrorized by
an evil entity that exploits the fears of its victims
to disguise itself while hunting its prey.
